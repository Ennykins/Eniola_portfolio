About Me

My journey into technology is as unique as it is inspiring. With a Bachelor's degree in Sociology, I was always fascinated by the impact of technology on society. This curiosity led me to dive deeper into the tech world, where I found my true passion.

I started my tech journey with a Full-Stack Bootcamp, where I honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various backend technologies. This foundational knowledge opened up new horizons, and I decided to further my education with a Master's degree in Computer Science. During this period, I delved into algorithms, data structures, and object-oriented programming.

My thirst for knowledge didn't stop there. I enrolled in a Data Analytics Bootcamp to master data manipulation, visualization, and analysis. This comprehensive journey equipped me with a diverse skill set, enabling me to tackle complex problems and deliver efficient solutions.

Additionally, my experience as a Project Manager has endowed me with excellent organizational and leadership skills, allowing me to manage projects effectively and deliver results on time.
